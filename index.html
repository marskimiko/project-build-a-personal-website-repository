Marisa Saunders is a producer & audio engineer engineer based in Brooklyn, New York. She graduated in 2019 from The New School with a degree in liberal arts. She also studied audio engineering at the Recording Workshop, and is currently enrolled at The Flatiron School. Marisa is currently doing freelance mixing and production, as well as mix assisting for Tony Maserati, and working part time at the Atlantic Records Studio. Marisa hopes to be working as a software engineer in the near future.
